The 50/50 AI Program

🚀 Achieve 50% More with 50% Less Effort

6 Weeks | 1 Hour per Week | One-on-One Mentorship
Price: AED 1,499 (125 AED / hour)

This summer, join students from across the world in a hands-on, high-impact program designed to help you do more with less — by unlocking the power of AI.

The 50/50 Program helps students boost productivity and performance — delivering 50% more results with 50% less effort. Through the smart and ethical use of AI tools, students learn how to work faster, think clearer, and present stronger.

🌟 What You Can Expect

  • 💡 Personalized support your personal projects Turn rough ideas into structured plans — whether it’s for a research paper, a presentation, or a personal goal — using AI to break down complex tasks step by step.
  • ✍️ Master AI for Smarter Schoolwork Use Notion AI and ChatGPT to organize your thoughts, build outlines, and edit drafts — while staying true to your voice and academic integrity.
  • 🎨 Standout in College Applications Design stunning visuals and presentations using Canva AI, Gamma, and DALL·E to enhance school projects, pitch decks, or application presentations.
  • 📚 Research Like a Pro Use tools like Perplexity, Elicit, and Scite to find credible sources and build stronger arguments — faster and more efficiently.

🧭 Personalized Mentorship

Every student is matched with a dedicated mentor for the full six weeks. Your mentor will support you during and between sessions — providing guidance, feedback, and encouragement throughout the program. Mentors with education from Wharton, Insead, IIT etc. with work experience from Uber, Amazon, Google etc.

🏅 Certificate of Completion

Students who successfully complete the program will receive a Real World Academy Certificate — a great addition to your academic portfolio, internship applications, or university file.
