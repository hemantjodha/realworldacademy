🚀 Real World Academy presents: The 50/50 Summer Program

6 Weeks | 1 Hour per Week | One-on-One Mentorship Included
Price: AED 1,499

This summer, join students from across the world in a hands-on, high-impact program designed to help you do more with less — by unlocking the power of AI.

The 50/50 Program helps students boost productivity and performance — delivering 50% more results with 50% less effort. Through the smart and ethical use of AI tools, students learn how to work faster, think clearer, and present stronger.

It’s perfect for tackling real academic tasks like:

  • ✍️ Writing essays
  • 🧠 Planning and completing summer projects
  • 🧾 Working on college or university applications
  • 📊 Preparing school presentations or coursework

Designed for students in Year 7 to Year 13, the 50/50 Program gives you the confidence and tools to stay ahead — in school and beyond.

🌟 What You’ll Learn

  • 💡 Think Bigger, Plan Smarter Turn rough ideas into structured plans — whether it’s for a research paper, a presentation, or a personal goal — using AI to break down complex tasks step by step.
  • 📚 Research Like a Pro Use tools like Perplexity, Elicit, and Scite to find credible sources and build stronger arguments — faster and more efficiently.
  • ✍️ Structure Without Stress Use Notion AI and ChatGPT to organize your thoughts, build outlines, and edit drafts — while staying true to your voice and academic integrity.
  • 🎨 Create Fast, Communicate Boldly Design stunning visuals and presentations using Canva AI, Gamma, and DALL·E to enhance school projects, pitch decks, or application presentations.

🧭 Personalized Mentorship

Every student is matched with a dedicated mentor for the full six weeks. Your mentor will support you during and between sessions — providing guidance, feedback, and encouragement throughout the program.

🏅 Certificate of Completion

Students who successfully complete the program will receive a Real World Academy Certificate — a great addition to your academic portfolio, internship applications, or university file.

📝 Program Details

  • ⏱ 1 hour per week, online
  • 📆 6-week summer program
  • 👥 One-on-one mentor support throughout
  • 🧑‍🎓 Open to all students from Year 7 to Year 13
  • 💰 Program fee: AED 1,499
