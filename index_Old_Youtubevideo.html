About Us

At Real World Academy, we are redefining education by bridging the gap between academics and real-world experience. Designed by Ivy League graduates and industry experts with backgrounds in venture capital, tech investing, and global tech giants like Apple and Meta, our programs provide students with hands-on learning opportunities in technology, entrepreneurship, and innovation.

Each program focuses on high-impact fields such as Artificial Intelligence, FinTech, and E-commerce, offering students the opportunity to explore cutting-edge industries through activities like startup visits, guest speaker sessions, and team-based business pitch challenges.

Why Choose Real World Academy?

  • Gain insights from experts actively shaping the tech landscape.
  • Participate in curated site visits to leading companies and innovation hubs.
  • Develop critical thinking, creativity, and entrepreneurial skills.
  • Elevate future college application profile with real-world experiences.
