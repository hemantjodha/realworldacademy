Brighton College Dubai

Brighton College is excited to launch a new learning initiative in partnership with Real World Academy


RWA is a forward-thinking program that bridges academics with entrepreneurship, offering students immersive exposure to high-growth tech sectors—starting with artificial intelligence. Through a series of engaging sessions, the Brighton community will gain essential AI skills and real-world insights.

The flagship program, “Building an AI Start-up,” is a one-week intensive running during the term break. Students will:
  • Discover cutting-edge AI tools like ChatGPT, Copilot, and MidJourney
  • Dive into real-world case studies and interactive sessions with industry experts
  • Visit innovative start-ups to see AI in action
  • Collaborate in teams to develop and pitch their own AI business ideas

Parents are also invited to special sessions that explore AI tools, its potential, and practical tips that they can use in their daily lives.

This is more than a course—it’s a launchpad for the next generation of entrepreneurs and innovators.
