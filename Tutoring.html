Frequently asked questions

What curriculums do you support?

We support IB, A-Levels, IGCSE, AP, SAT, and American curriculum across all grade levels.

Can I access recorded sessions?

Yes! Every session is automatically recorded and saved to your personal library for review.

How do I track my progress?

Your personal dashboard shows detailed progress reports after every session, including topics covered and areas for improvement.

How are tutors matched to students?

We carefully match tutors based on your curriculum, grade level, learning style, and academic goals.

What is the cancellation policy?

Sessions can be rescheduled or cancelled up to 24 hours in advance without penalty.

How do I start?

You can start by chatting with our advisor or booking your first trial session through the website.
